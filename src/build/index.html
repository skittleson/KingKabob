Menu

King Kabob is a fast-casual kabob restaurant and a full service catering provider in Fairfax, VA. We season our items with a special formulated mixed of in house spices for everyone's palate. Although Afghan food in general is very spicy, the food at King Kabob has been moderated so as to cater to a wider group of clientèle. We are a family friendly environment offering a special menu for children.

Wrapped Gyro
